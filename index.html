Setting the right load order for your mods is a crucial step to enjoying a stable modded game. BOSS can help with that, by providing automated load order sorting for over 30,000 mod plugins.

BOSS is simple to use, and provides thousands of plugin-specific messages, such as usage notes and buggy mod warnings, to help keep your game healthy. It's also fully customisable, so you can set it to sort plugins it doesn't already recognise, or sort those it does recognise in different places.

Millions of people worldwide use BOSS to help with their mod load orders. It's free and open source, so why not join them?
